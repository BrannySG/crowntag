# V1 movement and hit feel starting numbers

Playtesting the throwaway `prototypes/movement-hit-feel` branch accepted third-person **mouse look** with **camera-relative WASD** (free movement, not world-axis). Starting content tunables to seed `@crowntag/content`: move 8 m/s, sprint ×1.6, jump impulse 9, gravity 22, hit range 2.4 m, hit arc 90°, hit cooldown 0.35 s, stun 0.7 s, knockback 11. Exact values remain config-tweakable; this ADR locks the control model and the first playable baseline.
